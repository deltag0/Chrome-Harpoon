Thank you for downloading Chrome-Harpoon!

Chrome Harpoon is a minimalistic productivity-focused Chrome extension that helps you stay in flow by letting you quickly mark and jump to frequently used tabs with custom keybindings. Inspired by Harpoon on Neovim (check it out!), it's designed for anyone who wants faster tab navigation without reaching for the mouse.
If you sometimes struggle to find which tab you wanted to switch back to from your tens or even hundreds of tabs, this extension is for you!
It's also fully compatible with Vimium!

Instructions and Commands

To start using Chrome-Harpoon, here's a breakdown of the commands:

  • Alt A will mark/pin a tab
  • Alt 0-9 will switch to the marked tab (if existing), with 1 being the first marked tab and 0 the last
  • Alt Z will witch back to the previously selected tab
  • Alt Y will undo a tab switch (similar to Ctrl Y in text)

You can also click on the extension icon to modify your pinned tabs.
